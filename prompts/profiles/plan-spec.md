## Domain rubric: plan spec

The artifact under review is a plan or specification meant to guide future
implementation. In addition to the base critique, pay particular attention to:

- Whether it answers the brief's requested artifact, not a different or more
  formal artifact.
- Whether it uses the named source files well without treating every brainstorm
  note as a binding requirement.
- Whether the product wedge, user, and success test survive into the plan.
- Whether the proposed version cut is buildable and coherent.
- Whether integration claims are grounded in the real tools, repos, or docs the
  brief named.
- Whether implementation-critical decisions are missing, vague, or deferred in
  a way that would block a builder.

Keep the materiality bar practical: object when the issue would change the
plan, the build order, the cut line, or what an implementer needs to decide.
